Welcome to Cypress

At Cypress , we value both your safety and well being. Our goal is to provide a safety service to citizens of Toronto by allowing the users to report incidents or concerning issues within the city. By pin pointing the locations using our app of these reports we can send our team immediately to resolve the pending issues. Our application is available on our website's main page and is currently has developed for mobile users on android and iOS.How to reporting problems help us? Immediate reports can be analyzed by our team who are able to deploy our workers to investigate the issues. Problems will be prioritized by levels based on time of report and severity. More information about how our application works can be found here. Collection of improvements will create a better community and environment for our citizens to live in. We believe that every user will fall in love our user friendly design and welcome everyone to use our free services and hope you enjoy the experience!To reach these goals we provide the services needed to recognize your reported issues and ensure they are taken care of.
